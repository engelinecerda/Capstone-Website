Welcome to ELI Coffee Events Cafe

ELI Coffee Binangonan is a Filipino-owned café and event lounge located in Darangan, Binangonan, Rizal. The café offers specialty coffee, artisanal beverages, desserts, and light meals in a cozy and aesthetic setting that is popular for casual meetups, study sessions, and coffee dates. In addition to serving drinks and food, the café also provides lounge spaces that can be reserved for private gatherings, meetings, and small celebrations.
